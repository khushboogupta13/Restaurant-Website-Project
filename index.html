ABOUT US

With a rich history of over 15 years in Sydney, La Plage is home for all lip smacking cuisines you've come to love. More so than ever before, our main aim is that our customers truly appreciate the comfort and joy that is to be found in food. La Plage was named 2019 Hottest Restaurant and Hottest Dish in Australia by John Lethlean in the Weekend Australian Hot 50.
